---
title: Enterprise governance
description: Enterprise accounts and enterprise-team boundaries.
---

Enterprise is a cross-organization governance boundary, not a login actor or direct Repository owner. Enterprise teams
can span organizations and delegate organization access, enterprise roles, and licensing; organization teams remain
single-organization delegation boundaries. The approved first runtime slice owns Enterprise identity, Organization
affiliation, owner assignment and Repository visibility policy. Repository remains the final authorization owner.
Enterprise teams, managed users, billing, licensing and audit remain deferred.
