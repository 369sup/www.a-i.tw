# Account Context

## Purpose

Route analysis of account, namespace, membership, and team language.

## Summary

Account is a proposed strategic context. It owns account and membership facts but is not evidence of a runtime module or public contract.

## Rules

- Distinguish membership facts from authorization decisions.
- Require explicit contracts for consumers outside the owner context.
