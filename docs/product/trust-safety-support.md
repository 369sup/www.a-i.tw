# Trust, safety, support and qualification programs

狀態：Accepted research baseline / no runtime

These are separate capability families sharing one research document, not one Bounded Context.

| Family | Language |
| --- | --- |
| Trust & Safety | Terms, acceptable use, privacy, content removal, appeal, legal request, trade control |
| Support | Support plan, support ticket, publisher support and product feedback |
| Programs | Education eligibility, Campus Program, nonprofit eligibility and benefit grant |

Support ticket and product feedback are different lifecycle objects. Copilot-specific assistance and
Classroom code workflows are excluded.
