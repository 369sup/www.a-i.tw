# Billing and cost management

狀態：Accepted research baseline / no runtime

| Language | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Billing owner | Personal, Organization or Enterprise Account responsible for charges |
| Billing manager | Role allowed to administer billing |
| Subscription | Continuing commercial agreement |
| Invoice | Formal record of charges |
| Payment method | Card, invoice or external payment destination |
| Budget | Spending threshold and alert policy |
| Cost center | Financial allocation boundary across Users, Teams, Organizations or Repositories |
| Usage | Metered consumption fact |

Billing answers how cost is charged. Entitlement answers whether a capability is available. Cost center
does not grant resource access.
